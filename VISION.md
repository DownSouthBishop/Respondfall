
# Respondfall Vision Document

## The Problem

The average local service business misses 35-40% of inbound calls. They have no idea what that costs them because the loss is invisible.

The phone rings. Nobody answers. The caller moves on. No record. No follow-up. No recovery. Just revenue that evaporated silently.

## The Solution

Respondfall makes that loss visible and then eliminates it.

That $14,100 on your analytics screen is the entire sales argument. Not a feature list. Not a comparison chart. One number that represents money you already earned and almost lost.

## The Model

Everything runs once. Everything else runs itself.

### One-Time Human Setup

- **Product Build** — Claude Code builds the complete self-serve platform (onboarding, Twilio provisioning, Stripe billing, SMS sequences, AI qualification, analytics)
- **Pricing** — Bishop sets it once. Linda manages it indefinitely.
- **Sequences** — Written once, inherited by every new account, auto-populated with client details
- **Ad Creative** — Set once. Linda monitors performance and flags what needs to change.
- **White Label Template** — Built once. Every partner gets their brand skin from one config file.

### What Linda Runs

**The Outreach Engine**
- 2,000 prospects contacted per day across email, SMS, LinkedIn
- Sourced from Google Maps, Yelp, local directories
- Enriched with business data and scored by missed call likelihood
- 5% click-through to trial page = 100 trials per day

**The Conversion Engine**
- Self-serve onboarding in 4 minutes
- Revenue protected demo populated with their specific industry numbers
- Twilio number provisioned automatically
- Free trial with daily performance updates

**The Retention Engine**
- Weekly revenue protection numbers
- Monthly performance reports (first of month, every month)
- Quarterly business reviews for Agency tier
- Annual plan offers at month 6

**The Expansion Engine**
- Pro upgrades offered at day 45 with specific revenue opportunity
- Agency upgrades offered at month 3
- Skyforge website offers when site is weak
- Custom widget offers when use case is identified

**The Partner Engine**
- Continuous white label outreach to call centers and franchises
- Automatic white label demo provisioning
- Linda manages contracts up to approval threshold
- Partner onboarding and account health monitoring
